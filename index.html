Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Objectives and Rules

  1. The first player to get 3 of their symbols in a row, vertically, horizontally, or diagonally will win!
  2. If the board is full and there are no lines of 3 identical symbols, there are no winners.
  3. A player can click on an empty square only once and cannot change their symbol placement.
  4. Enter your names and get playing!
